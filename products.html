How To Use
01
Remove chapati from freezer. Let it thaw on a dry cloth until no longer frozen.
02
Heat a non-stick tawa on low flame. Place chapati on tawa.
03
Cook 20 sec, flip and cook 30 sec. Max 3 flips. Press gently to puff.
04
Add a little ghee or butter. Serve hot with dal, sabzi or curry.

Fry Time
How To Use
01
Take puris out of the freezer. Fry immediately — do not thaw.
02
Heat oil in a deep pan to medium flame (~180°C).
03
Slide puri into hot oil. Press lightly to puff. Fry golden on both sides.
04
Drain on paper. Serve hot with subzi, chutney or halwa.
