How To Use
01
Remove from freezer. Let thaw on a dry cloth until no longer frozen.
02

03
Cook 20 sec, flip, cook 30 sec. Max 3 flips. Press gently to puff.

Take puris straight from the freezer. Do not thaw — fry immediately.
