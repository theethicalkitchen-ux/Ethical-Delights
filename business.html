Operational Simplicity

Freezer to Table
in Three Steps

01
❄️
Thaw

Remove from the deep freezer. For chapatis, thaw on a dry cloth for a few minutes. For puris and bhaturas, go straight to the oil — no thaw needed.

2–5 minutes · No skill required
02
🔥
Heat

Chapatis: 60 seconds on a hot tawa — any team member can do it. Puris and Bhaturas: 10–15 seconds in hot oil. Perfectly golden, perfectly puffed, every time.

60 seconds · Any team member
03
✅
Serve

Soft, aromatic, and fresh-tasting. Your guests can't tell the difference from freshly made. Cook only what you need — put the rest back in the freezer. Zero wastage.

Serve immediately · Zero wastage
